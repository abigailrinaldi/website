About

Abigail Rinaldi is an actress and model whose work blends cinematic storytelling with modern fashion imagery. Drawn to emotionally rich performances and visually compelling campaigns, she approaches every project with curiousity, professionalism, and creative versatality.


Her interests span film, television, editorial, beauty, runway, and commercial work. She enjoys collaborating with artists and brands that value thoughtful storytelling and timeless aesthetics.


Currently based in New York City and available for projects worldwide.
